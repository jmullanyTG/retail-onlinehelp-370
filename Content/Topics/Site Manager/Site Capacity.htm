Site Capacity

The site capacity view allows users to view and update site capacity information within JustEnough. This data is not consumed within any ordering operations and is used for reporting and filtering only.

  • Hierarchy Level – The specified hierarchy level at which capacity will be measured for the site. The hierarchy used is controlled through a system policy setting.
  • Level – The level value within the product dimension. Will associate directly with the hierarchy levels.
  • Selling Capacity – Showroom capacity in quantity of units.
  • Stockroom Capacity – Stockroom capacity in quantity of units.
  • Minimum Capacity – Minimum capacity for the total site.
  • Maximum Capacity – Maximum capacity for the total site.
  • Active – A flag displaying the active status of the capacity constraint.
  • Created Date – Date of creation for the capacity entry.
  • Created By – User credited with the creation.
  • Last Updated – Date of the most recent update to the capacity entry.
  • Last Update By – User credited with the most recent update.
