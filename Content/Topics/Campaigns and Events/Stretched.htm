Stretched

Setting the docking property to be stretched vertically or horizontally forces the container to fill all the available vertical or horizontal space and constrains the content to the container size. 

Images will be forced to fit proportionally to the container, while text will wrap within the container until it runs out of space at which point any text after that will be cut off.  Unlike containers that are aligned to the top, bottom, left, right or center, stretched containers to not shrink or expand based on content.
